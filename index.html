Organic and Fresh Products

Ingredients used in the delicacies is grown organically without harmful pesticides, which are then handpicked from the local produce market and consumed within 2-3 days to make sure they are freshly used and served.

Excellent Service

Our staff here in dawn cafe are trained well to produce and serve efficiently. Baristas are trained for 3 months, and the pastry chefs are well versed with their art.

Hygienic and Healthy

Regular inspection of cafe cleanliness is conducted along with which the kitchen is frequently sanitised. We try to reduce the waste produced, and decompose naturally. Drinking and Smoking are prohibited in the cafe.
