Iced Americano

Espresso shots topped with cold water produce a light layer of crema, then served over ice.

Macchiato

Prepared traditionally with one shot of espresso and a dollop of steamed milk and foam on top of the espresso.

Ristretto

A more concentrated and darker version of the espresso, espresso shot, prepared with only half of the amount of water.

Café Latte

Served in a cup with 1 shot of espresso, steamed milk and 1 cm micro-foam on top of the milk, giving it a sweet flavor.

Cappuccino

Similar to the latte, the difference is that a Cappuccino has more foam (2-3 cm) on top, and chocolate sprinkle on top.

Flat White

Similar to the Cappuccino, the only difference between the two of them is that a Flat White doesn’t have any foam.

Piccolo Latte

Piccolo Latte consists in a shot of espresso served on an espresso cup, with steamed milk and a tiny portion of foam.

Mocha

Mocha consists in a shot of espresso mixed with a spoon of chocolate powder, on top you have to add steamed milk and 2-3 cm of foam, finally some sprinkles of chocolate powder.

Affogato

Affogato is a dessert made of a single or double shot of espresso served over a scoop of vanilla ice-cream. It is served in a tumbler glass. 12. Irish Coffee
